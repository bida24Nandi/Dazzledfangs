The History of Tooth Gems

Tooth gems, small decorative crystals adhered to teeth, originated in the 1990s as a trend in the hip-hop and celebrity culture. Inspired by flashy dental accessories popularized by musicians, especially rappers, they became symbols of luxury and individuality.
