The history of tooth gems

Tooth gems, small decorative crystals adhered to teeth, originated in the 1990s as a trend in the hip-hop and celebrity culture. Inspired by the flashy dental accessories popularized by musicians, especially rappers, these gems became a symbol of luxury and individuality. Over time, tooth gems gained popularity beyond the music industry, spreading to various subcultures.
